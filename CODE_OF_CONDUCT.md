# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Heard to be a useful, serious, high-quality open-source project.

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, work constructively, and focus on improving the software.

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- Be direct and specific.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Welcome new contributors.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or threats.
- Personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.
- Bad-faith behavior that undermines the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the project maintainers through the official project channels.

Reports will be reviewed as privately and fairly as possible.
